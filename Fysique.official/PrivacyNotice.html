    Privacy Notice

    Last Updated: March 26, 2026

    At Fysique Apparel, we value your privacy and your hustle. This notice explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you shop our gear or visit our site.

    I. Information We Collect

    We collect data to get your fitness apparel from our screens to your doorstep:

    Order Info: Name, shipping/billing address, and phone number.

    Payment Info: Processed securely via 256-bit SSL encryption, PCI DSS Level 1 compliance, and integrated fraud analysis tools; we do not store your full credit card details.

    Technical Data: IP address, device type, and "cookies" to help our store run smoothly.

    Fit Preferences: Any sizing or style preferences you provide to help us recommend the right gear

    II. Why We Need Your Data

    We use your information to:

    Fulfill and ship your orders.

    Screen for potential risk or fraud.

    Send you workout-ready updates and offers (only if you opt-in!).

    III. How We Share Data

    To run Fysique Apparel, we share your data with essential partners for shipping and handling:

    Fulfillment Partners: Your name and address are shared with our external suppliers solely to pack and ship your specific order.

    Analytics: We use tools like Google Analytics to see which styles you love most.

    IV. Your Rights (Global & 2026 Standards)

    Regardless of where you live, we respect your right to:

    Access & Portability: Request a copy of the data we hold.

    Correction/Deletion: Ask us to fix or wipe your info.

    Opt-Out: Stop targeted ads or tracking via "Global Privacy Control" (GPC) signals.

    Minors: We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18 without parental consent.

    V. Data Retention

    Fysique Apparel is committed to protecting the privacy of our users while maintaining necessary records for operational, legal, and regulatory purposes. This Data Retention Policy outlines our practices regarding the storage, retention, and deletion of personal data.

    The purpose of this policy is to:

    Ensure compliance with applicable laws.

    Optimize storage resources by removing unnecessary data.

    Ensure personal data is not kept longer than necessary.

    VI. Contact Us

    For any questions about your data or to exercise your rights, please contact:

    Email: fysique.apparel@gmail.com
